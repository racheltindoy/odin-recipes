Carbonara

(Simple pasta with egg and cheese)

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • 1 egg
  • Grated cheese (parmesan preferred)
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook pasta. Save some pasta water.
  2. Whisk egg + cheese in a bowl.
  3. Add hot pasta to egg mix off the heat (to avoid scrambling).
  4. Stir quickly until creamy. Add pepper.
